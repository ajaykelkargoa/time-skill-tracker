Time-Skill Alignment Tracker

A leadership coaching tool to analyze where you invest your time, energy, and attention.

How to Use This Tool:

  1. Track all major activities from your past week
  2. For each activity, note: hours spent, your skill level, strategic impact, and energy levels
  3. Review the analysis to identify perfectionism patterns and delegation opportunities
  4. Discuss insights with your coach
